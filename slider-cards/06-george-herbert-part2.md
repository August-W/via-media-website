Drew nearer to me, sweetly questioning
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If I lacked anything.
"You must sit down," says Love, "and taste my meat."
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;So I did sit and eat.

citation: Divine Poems (1633)

Batter my heart, three-person'd God; for you
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As yet but knock, breathe, shine, and seek to mend;
That I may rise, and stand, o'erthrow me, and bend
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Your force to break, blow, burn, and make me new.

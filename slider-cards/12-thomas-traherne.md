---
title: Thomas Traherne (1636–1674)
subtitle: Anglican priest, poet, and mystic of the Caroline tradition
type: quote
card_number: 12
citation: Centuries of Meditations (1908)
---

"You never enjoy the world aright, till the Sea itself floweth in your veins, till you are clothed with the heavens, and crowned with the stars... Till your spirit filleth the whole world, and the stars are your jewels."

poem_title: "A Better Resurrection"
citation: Verses (1893)

My life is like a frozen thing,
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;No bud nor greenness can I see:
Yet rise it shall—the sap of Spring;
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;O Jesus, rise in me.

---
title: Jeremy Taylor (1613–1667)
subtitle: Bishop and Caroline divine, author of Holy Living and Holy Dying
type: quote
card_number: 15
citation: The Rule and Exercises of Holy Living (1650)
---

"God hath given to man a short time here upon earth, and yet upon this short time eternity depends. He that is choice of his time will also be choice of his company, and choice of his actions."

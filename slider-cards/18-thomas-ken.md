---
title: Thomas Ken (1637–1711)
subtitle: Bishop of Bath and Wells and author of the Doxology
type: poem
card_number: 18
poem_title: "Morning Hymn"
citation: Manual of Prayers for Winchester Scholars (1695)
---

Awake, my soul, and with the sun
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Thy daily stage of duty run;
Shake off dull sloth, and joyful rise
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To pay thy morning sacrifice.
